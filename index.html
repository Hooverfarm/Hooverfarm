- /e Server Hop {Hops to a 4 Player or lower server.}
- /e Sell Items {Sells all items enabled in the config.}
- /e Shift 1-3 {Shifts your speed for below command.}
- /e Toggle Speed {Toggles speed based off shift.}
- /e Teleport A {Teleports you to Arcade.}
- /e ESP Toggle {Toggles ESP (Synapse only).}
- /e Shiny Farm {Be standless and type command}
- /e Arcade roll {Rolls max you can afford in arcade.}
- /e Invisible {Hides your "Body" by teleporting it}
- /e Auto Quest {Automatically levels you to max.}
